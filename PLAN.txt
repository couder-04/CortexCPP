One important change from the old roadmap

I would move:

ParameterCounter
ArchitectureValidator
ModelSummary
LayerFactory

before FLOPs and Memory Analysis.

These features provide much more value early and make the CLI far more useful. 🚀

You’re currently around Milestone 2 (60–70% complete) since Tensor, Sequential, Conv2D, MaxPool2D, Linear, ReLU, Flatten, and Softmax already exist.
